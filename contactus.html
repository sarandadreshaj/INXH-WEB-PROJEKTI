Delivery

Delivery Wordery offers FREE worldwide delivery on ALL orders.

Standard UK orders are sent via Royal Mail and selected orders, such as Express Delivery, via partner couriers.

All international orders are sent via Royal Mail priority air mail.

We will always try to send your order in as few packages as possible. However sometimes we have to send your items separately, but we'll display this clearly in My Wordery orders.

Delivery Times

The expected delivery time for each item is clearly listed on each product page and in checkout.

Postal services vary around the world and delivery speed depends on the delivery country and the item you are ordering.

As a guide most items will be delivered in the following times:

  • United Kingdom 3-5 working days

  • Europe 7-10 working days

  • USA and Canada up to 21 working days

  • Australia 7-10 working days

  • Rest of the World 7-14 working days

Express Delivery

EXPRESS DELIVERY currently offers a tracked express courier courier service:

Tracked Express Courier £3.00 per order - Guaranteed and tracked (excludes sundays and public holidays).
Books stocked in our warehouse that are being delivered to mainland UK are eligible for delivery upgrades, and can be identified by the EXPRESS DELIVERY tag.


Why can't I upgrade all of the items in my order?

Some of our books are print-on-demand, or sourced from around the globe, so they can’t be upgraded to EXPRESS DELIVERY .
The time it takes us to get these books from our suppliers is already included in the delivery estimates we show on our product pages, and in checkout.


Why has my order been split into two delivery types?

This is to make it clear which books will be sent using our standard free worldwide delivery and those that eligible for EXPRESS DELIVERY upgrades. We always try to send you as few parcels as possible.


Why can't I upgrade my delivery to outside the UK?

Royal Mail First Class and our current courier only deliver to mainland UK. We'll let you know if this changes in the future.


Are there any places in the UK where delivery cannot be made or where delivery is slower?

Northern Ireland and Scottish Highlands and Islands are usually a 2-3 working day service for Royal Mail First Class deliveries. For Tracked Courier, it’s a 2 working day service. Unfortunately, we don't currently offer EXPRESS DELIVERY to Gibraltar, Guernsey or Jersey.


Royal Mail First Class

  • Mon – Fri: order by 13:00 for next working day delivery (Northern Ireland, and the Scottish Highlands and Islands are usually a 2-3 working day service).

  • Sat – Sun: delivery will be on Tuesday (excluding public holidays).

  • Order on a public holiday and your order may take an extra working day to arrive.

  • Royal Mail does not deliver on public holidays or Sundays.


How do I track my order?

Tracking is only available for tracked express courier. We’ll email you a tracking link when your delivery is on the way. Our courier usually collects parcels the evening before, or on the day of, your delivery.
First Class and free delivery are not tracked services.


Tracked Express Courier

Guaranteed and tracked courier delivery.

  • Mon - Thurs: order by 17:00 for next working day delivery (15:00 for Northern Ireland and Scottish Highlands and Islands – this is a two working day service).

  • Fri - Sun: order by 10:00am on Sun for next working day delivery.

  • Sun and bank holidays: orders will be delivered in two working days.

  • Our courier does not deliver on public holidays or Saturdays and Sundays.

Card Authorisation

In order for our payment provider to check that your credit / debit card details are correct and valid they perform a verification check on your card. Occasionally this involves a small (£/$/€ 1) authorisation being generated, which may appear on your statement. Please note, the money will not actually be taken from your account and this charge should disappear or be reversed within a couple of days. If you believe this charge has not been correctly reversed, please use the Contact Us section and we can look into the issue further.
